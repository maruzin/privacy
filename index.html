Privacy Policy

SnaggIt Pro — Professional Construction Snagging & Defect Management

Effective Date: April 19, 2026

1. Introduction

SnaggIt Pro ("we", "our", "the App") is a professional construction snagging and defect management application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our snagging and defect management services, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and name when you create an account or sign in via Google or Apple.
  • Project Data: Project names, descriptions, addresses, and snagging reports you create within the App.
  • Photos & Camera: Images captured through the in-app camera for documenting construction snags and defects. Photos are stored on your device and synced to our secure cloud servers.
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates may be attached to snag entries to record the location of defects, only when you grant location permission.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting and compatibility purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the snagging and defect management service.
  • Sync your projects, snags, and reports across devices.
  • Generate PDF reports of snagging inspections.
  • Enable team collaboration and sharing of project data.
  • Improve the App's functionality and user experience.
  • Provide customer support.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud infrastructure provider with enterprise-grade security. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest. The App also stores data locally on your device for offline access, using encrypted local storage.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • Team Members: Project data is shared with team members you explicitly invite to a project.
  • Service Providers: We use Supabase for cloud hosting and Google/Apple for authentication. These providers have their own privacy policies.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

6. Permissions

The App requests the following device permissions:

  • Camera: To photograph construction snags and defects.
  • Location: To geo-tag snag entries (optional).
  • Storage/Photos: To save and access snag photographs.
  • Internet: To sync data with the cloud and enable collaboration.
  • Notifications: To send reminders about pending snags and project updates.

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed from our servers within 30 days. Project data shared with team members may be retained as part of shared projects.

8. Children's Privacy

SnaggIt Pro is a professional tool intended for use by adults in the construction industry. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Export your project data.
  • Withdraw consent for optional data collection (e.g., location).

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
